🔍 How HealthRisk AI Works

A simple explanation of what goes on behind the scenes.

📥 1. You Fill Out the Form

You enter your age, gender, blood pressure, cholesterol, and select your lifestyle habits (like smoking or drinking).

🧼 2. Your Data Gets Processed

  • Gender and cholesterol are turned into numbers (encoding)
  • All values are scaled to help the model perform better
  • Habits are converted into a “risk point” based on what you selected

🧠 3. PyTorch Model Makes a Prediction

We use a simple PyTorch linear model that calculates your health risk based on your processed data.

Fun fact: Your habits score is boosted by 1.1x to give it more influence in the prediction. Lifestyle matters!

📊 4. You Get a Result + Confidence

The model gives a prediction like "High Risk" or "Low Risk", along with a confidence percentage (unless it’s unsure).

📝 5. Personalized Recommendations

If we detect high-risk factors like smoking or high blood pressure, we’ll show helpful suggestions to improve your health.

🔮 6. What-If Simulator

If we mention smoking or drinking in the recommendations, you'll see a simulator that lets you adjust your habits and see how your risk might change — all in real-time using JavaScript.

⚠️ This tool is for educational and awareness purposes only. Not actual medical advice.
